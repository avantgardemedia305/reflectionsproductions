Nights the City
Remembers Remembers

From Independence Day fireworks to the New Year's countdown, Reflections stages carry South Florida's signature celebrations. Here's what that looks like.

Milander Park, Hialeah · July 4

Hialeah
4th of July

The City of Hialeah's Independence Day celebration draws thousands to Milander Park — headlined by Cuban reggaeton duo Gente de Zona, closed by one of the biggest fireworks shows in the state. Reflections handled the production end to end, load-in to strike, coordinating artists, city officials and vendors.

  • APEX GEN3 hydraulic mobile stage
  • Full JBL concert audio, FOH & monitors
  • Intelligent lighting rig, designed & operated
  • Show power, barriers & site logistics

Bayfront Park, Miami · Dec 31

Big Orange
NYE Miami

Miami's answer to the ball drop: the 35-foot Big Orange climbing the InterContinental as tens of thousands count down at Bayfront Park. The free Countdown Miami festival has carried names like Marshmello, Nicky Jam, Flo Rida, Gente de Zona and Toño Rosario — with Reflections building the show beneath them.

  • Reflections mobile staging
  • LED video walls, live camera feeds & switching
  • Concert audio & lighting through midnight
  • Full production & crew, into the new year

Read the full story

Miami E-Prix · 2025 Season

Formula E
Fan Village

When the ABB FIA Formula E World Championship came to Miami, the Fan Village stage was an APEX 2016 — wrapped edge to edge in a custom branded arch package, with an LED screen running race-day content and JBL sound covering the village.

  • APEX 2016 mobile stage, custom-branded
  • Full-width LED video screen
  • JBL sound system for the fan village
  • Compact footprint on a tight race-campus site
Floyd, Virginia · Festival Season

FloydFest

Proof the Princeton yard travels: two APEX stages rolled up the Blue Ridge for FloydFest — a GEN3 and the 2424 side by side on the festival lawn, running alternating sets so the music never stopped between changeovers.

  • APEX GEN3 + APEX 2424, dual-stage build
  • LED walls & lighting on both decks
  • Back-to-back changeovers, zero dead air
  • Long-haul trucking & tour logistics

Bahia Mar, Fort Lauderdale · Feb 7–9, 2023

Mobile Stage
Conference '23

The largest mobile-stage networking event of the year, and Reflections was at the center of it — three days of training on sales, rentals and safety at the Bahia Mar Hotel, where the APEX 5040 GEN3 turned heads for its structural integrity and lightning-fast deployment.

  • APEX 5040 GEN3 showcase build
  • Walk-the-deck rigging & outrigger demos
  • Safety, DOT & certification forums
  • Feb 7–9, 2023 · Fort Lauderdale, FL

Nationwide · Live Broadcast

Network
Broadcast Sets

The setup used by ESPN nationwide for College GameDays: our APEX 3224 is the same stage the major sports networks use for their “live on the scene” sets — auto racing, horse racing and college football — placed and broadcast-ready within 30 minutes, roof at variable heights.

  • APEX 3224 broadcast-set configuration
  • “MyPad” remote studio & control room
  • HD cameras, grips & operators
  • Set in 30 minutes, roof at variable trim
