About

Half a Century of Showtime

’75Princeton, Florida — then, now, always

Reflections Productions started in 1975 as a South Florida production shop with a simple idea: if the show matters, everything behind it should come from people who've done it before. Fifty years later, that idea has grown into a family of brands — Reflections Productions, Mobile Stages Miami and the APEX stage line.

Today the company runs eight APEX hydraulic mobile stages from its Princeton, Florida yard, backed by concert audio, lighting, video and a crew that has built shows for cities, festivals, broadcasters and touring acts across the region.

Three generations of South Florida audiences have watched their favorite nights happen on our decks. We plan on a fourth.
