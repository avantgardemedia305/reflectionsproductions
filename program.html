The agreement.

Twelve months, everything we've walked through, in plain language. Sign below — typed or drawn — then save the PDF for your records. Full standing policies live at avantgardelaunchpad.com/terms; this document reflects them.

01 · Parties & term

This Services Agreement is between Avant Garde Launchpad (Avant Garde Media — “AGL”) and Reflections Productions Inc (“Client”), effective on the date of the last signature below. Initial term: twelve (12) months. Before renewal, the parties hold a year-two review built on the monthly scoreboard. After month 12 the agreement auto-renews month-to-month at the same rate unless either party gives 30 days' written notice.

02 · Scope

Launch work: Brand Identity (logo suite, palette, typography, usage kit) and Bespoke Website build (21 pages, stage + production-service spec pages, 3D stage viewer, quote funnel, launch: forms, redirects, analytics). Mission retainer, monthly: 4 blog posts · 20 social posts + 8 reels · 1 content shoot (Client's pick) · Google Business Profile + email management · outreach, bid monitoring, and reporting per the Mission tab · bi-weekly strategy calls. Work outside scope (rush turnaround, new builds, strategic projects) is quoted first as a change order, approved in writing, then billed — nothing is a surprise.

03 · Investment

Standard pricing: Brand Identity $5,000 + Bespoke Website $10,000 + Mission $5,000/month × 12 = $75,000. With the twelve-month retainer commitment, launch work is 50% off per AGL's published rule: launch $7,500 · year one $67,500.

04 · Payment & the barter structure

(a) Cash: $2,500/month, first payment August 1, 2026, then the same calendar day monthly; due on invoice. (b) Trade credits: the remaining balance — $7,500 launch + $2,500/month = $37,500 for year one — is payable in trade credits earned on AGL-originated production jobs (including The Underline concert series). Per job: Client's documented hard costs (crew labor, delivery, fuel at actual) are paid to Client in cash, off the top, within five (5) business days of AGL receiving the event payment; the margin above hard costs credits against Client's balance until the $37,500 is fully funded; thereafter Client retains 85% of collected gross on AGL-originated jobs and AGL receives a 15% origination commission. (c) Credits are earned on completed and invoiced events only; if the $7,500 launch portion is not fully covered by credits within ninety (90) days of signing, the unfunded launch balance is invoiced in cash. (d) AGL issues a monthly statement and credit memo in Client's Mission Control portal. (e) The $5,000 monthly commitment is unconditional and scope never reduces: in any month where earned credit does not cover the $2,500 barter portion, the difference is due in cash. (f) Trade is recorded at fair market value; each party is responsible for its own tax reporting. (g) AGL-originated events run under Client's standard production terms, except deposit and prepayment terms, which this agreement supersedes.

05 · À la carte, media & travel

Client receives 30% off published à la carte rates for the full term. Ad media is billed at cost as a separate pass-through (no markup) once the ads phase begins. Merch and print production at cost +15%, design included. Travel within 30 miles of Miami is included; beyond that, travel bills at $1/mile round-trip, lodging at cost, and a $75/day per diem.

06 · Plan changes

Per published policy: upgrade any time; downgrade only at renewal. The twelve-month commitment applies to the Mission tier or above.

07 · Early cancellation

Why twelve months: growth compounds — one month of marketing doesn't change anything, and the launch discount is priced on the full-year commitment. Convenience cancellation before month 12: remaining months become due at the non-discounted rate and the launch discount ($7,500 differential) becomes payable; credits already earned still count. Major life events (business closure or sale, owner illness, force majeure) are handled case-by-case — pause and re-engage preferred; we don't sue clients. AGL may terminate for material breach, including non-payment past 30 days. Late payments follow AGL's published ladder: reminder at 5 days · 1.5%/month at 15 · suspension at 30 · termination and collections at 60+ · $50 NSF per returned payment.

08 · Ownership

Client owns the work: final brand identity files, website code and assets, edited photos, finished videos, published copy — perpetual usage rights, originals delivered quarterly, and full handoff of everything if the relationship ever ends. AGL retains raw files and source materials (licensable separately at published rates) and the right to showcase the work in its portfolio and case studies (mutual NDA addendum available on request).

09 · Confidentiality, access & force majeure

Client data is never sold or shared. AGL receives working admin access (site, Google Business Profile, socials, analytics, ad platforms) for the term; access is removed at contract end; any security incident is disclosed within 24 hours. Force majeure (natural disaster, mandated closures, infrastructure outage, government acts): the affected party notifies within 48 hours, obligations and billing pause, and either party may exit without penalty if the event continues 60+ days.

10 · General

Targets and projections in the accompanying tabs are models, not guarantees — the monthly scoreboard reports actuals. AGL's liability is capped at fees paid in the preceding three months. Governing law: Florida; venue Miami-Dade County (courts or binding arbitration in Miami). Typed, drawn, or electronic signatures are binding. This agreement, together with the Mission, Payment Plan, and À La Carte tabs it accompanies, is the entire agreement; standing policies are published at avantgardelaunchpad.com/terms.
