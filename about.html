The Story

Built Show
by Show

’75Princeton, Florida — then, now, always

Reflections Productions started in 1975 as a South Florida production shop — lighting, sound and staging for whoever had a show that needed doing right. The company earned its tagline, “South Florida's Creative Source,” the slow way: one city celebration, one festival, one touring act at a time.

As the shows grew, so did the shop. Stage rentals became their own operation — Mobile Stages Miami — and the arrival of the APEX hydraulic stage line turned a staging company into a fleet operator. Today those names are a family of brands consolidated under one roof: same yard, same crew, same phone number.

Fifty years in, the company is still family-run from Princeton, Florida. Three generations of South Florida audiences have watched their favorite nights happen on our decks. We plan on a fourth.

The Yard

Princeton,
Florida

23750SW 132 Avenue · Princeton, FL 33032

Everything on this site lives in one place: a working yard in Princeton, Florida, south of Miami, where the stages, trucks and inventory stage out to shows across the state and up the coast.

The logistics operation is its own department — CDL drivers and trucking, MultiQuip diesel generators from 25 to 125 kW with festival power distribution, the secure “MyPad” control-room motorhome, FOH trailers, Bil Jax event scaffolding, French barricades, cable ramps, and event credentialing down to custom photo ID badges, security shirts and uniforms.
