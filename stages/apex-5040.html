Overview

The APEX 5040 is the flagship of our fleet, fitted with the technology that makes it one of the fastest, strongest and safest mobile stages in the industry. Four distinct hydraulic systems handle the entire deployment — outriggers for stability, towers for the roof, roof tilt for the primary and secondary roof, and a floor system that folds the deck from vertical to horizontal.

Pair it with our 48-box JBL VTX V25-II line array, the Absen X5 LED wall and our intelligent lighting inventory, and you have a serious concert rig for an audience of 20,000 plus — a world-class touring setup we can have ready at a moment's notice.
