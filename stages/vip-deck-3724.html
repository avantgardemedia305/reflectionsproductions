Overview

The Captain's VIP Deck 3724 is a high-performance mobile platform engineered for rapid deployment at concerts, festivals and large public gatherings. Fully deployed it expands to 45 by 24 feet — roughly 888 square feet of usable space for VIP viewing, hospitality, DJ platforms or production positions.

For transport it folds to 35 feet long, 102 inches wide and 10 feet 6 inches tall — fully DOT road-legal with no oversize permits. The multi-bay deck expands symmetrically around a reinforced structural spine, with an upper viewing level, integrated stairs, landing platforms and full-perimeter guardrails. It supports ADA-compliant access points, and the structure is IBC, FBC and NFPA life-safety compliant. U.S. Patent #US12084881 B1.
