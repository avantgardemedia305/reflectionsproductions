Overview

The APEX 2016 proves a compact stage doesn't have to feel like one. The fit and finish are excellent, the build is solid, and it flies 800 pounds on each flybay with a total roof capacity of 3,400 pounds — real production capability in a small footprint.

The stage opens on the front side and comes with aluminum stairs and a removable modular back panel — the lower panels can stay in place as handrails or come out entirely. Easy to operate, maneuverable in tight areas and budget-friendly, it maintains APEX quality at community-event scale. An optional banner package is available.

You've already seen this stage around town: most recently dressed as the Formula E Fan Village stage at the 2025 Miami E-Prix, wrapped in a custom branded arch with a full-width LED screen. See it in Our Work
