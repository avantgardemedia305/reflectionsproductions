24′ × 20′ DECK Hydraulic deployment

Overview

The APEX 2420 is the perfect stage for smaller-venue professional productions, corporate shows and non-profit events. It tows behind a one-ton truck with a gooseneck hitch, which keeps transport simple and site access flexible.

Don't let the footprint fool you: each flybay holds an impressive 2,000 pounds, and the enclosed trailer secures an additional 4,000 pounds of cargo on the way in — sound, lighting or backline riding with the stage.
