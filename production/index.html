JBL Line Arrays

Concert Audio

Concert-quality sound leaves no room for compromise. We run JBL professional line arrays — VTX A12, VTX V25-II, SRX900 and powered VRX systems — scaled from a city block party to a 20,000-person festival field.

AEO Mojo · Front-of-Stage

Crowd Control & MOJO Barriers

Concert crowds generate serious forward pressure. AEO Mojo barriers — 6063-T6 aluminum, ISO 9001 and CE certified — absorb and distribute that force while giving security a stable, anti-slip platform to monitor the front row and reach anyone who needs help.

Gates, corners and cable pass-through sections configure into camera pits, VIP zones and restricted areas. We plan the barrier layout with your security team so the front row stays safe and the fire marshal stays happy — and we carry French barricades and cable ramps for the rest of the site.

AEO Mojo barrier (model AEO-K-P1) · 6063-T6 aluminum · step platforms
Sections
Straights · gates · corners · cable pass-throughs
Site kit
French barricades · cable ramps · custom photo ID credentials
